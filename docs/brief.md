# Tidal Ink Tattoo Studio Brief

## Business Overview
Tidal Ink Tattoo Studio is a premium tattoo studio based in Mount Maunganui, New Zealand. The studio offers custom tattooing with a focus on refined artistry, a calm client experience, and high-end presentation.

## Project Goal
Create a modern, premium website that positions Tidal Ink as a trusted, artistic, and professional tattoo studio. The site should drive enquiries and bookings while showcasing artists and portfolio work.

## Audience
- Adults in Mount Maunganui, Tauranga, and the wider Bay of Plenty
- Visitors seeking custom tattoo work
- Clients comparing artist styles before booking
- Customers looking for a clean, professional, premium studio

## Brand Direction
- Dark, modern aesthetic
- Gold accent colour for a premium feel
- Clean typography
- Editorial-style layouts with strong imagery and negative space
- Tone: confident, artistic, welcoming, precise

## Required Pages
1. Home
2. Artists
3. Gallery
4. Booking
5. Contact

## Key Content Requirements
### Home
- Clear brand introduction
- Hero with strong statement and CTA
- Overview of studio values and tattoo specialties
- Featured artists preview
- Gallery preview
- Booking CTA

### Artists
- Individual artist sections/cards
- Style specialties
- Short bios
- Experience and approach
- CTA to book with preferred artist

### Gallery
- Curated portfolio categories or highlights
- Emphasis on craftsmanship and detail
- Clear browsing structure
- CTA to book after viewing work

### Booking
- Consultation-first booking process
- What clients should prepare
- Steps from idea to appointment
- Deposit and policy summary
- Booking CTA or enquiry path

### Contact
- Studio address in Mount Maunganui, New Zealand
- Hours
- Email/contact method
- Social links placeholder text if real links unavailable
- Map/location context

## Business Details for Site Copy
- Location: Mount Maunganui, New Zealand
- Positioning: premium custom tattoo studio
- Services: custom tattoos, fine line, blackwork, illustrative, consultations
- Values: hygiene, artistry, collaboration, client comfort

## Conversion Goals
- Encourage booking enquiries
- Help visitors choose an artist
- Build trust through quality presentation and clear process

## SEO Focus
- Tattoo studio Mount Maunganui
- Tattoo artists Mount Maunganui
- Custom tattoos Tauranga
- Premium tattoo studio Bay of Plenty

## Accessibility and UX
- Strong contrast on dark backgrounds
- Clear nav and CTAs
- Responsive layout across mobile, tablet, desktop
- Semantic HTML and descriptive alt text
